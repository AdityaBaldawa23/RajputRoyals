Our Amenities

Banquet Hall

Our banquet hall is the perfect venue for all your special occasions, combining elegance, comfort, and luxury to create unforgettable memories. Adorned with stunning chandeliers that add a touch of grandeur, our hall features customizable backdrops and décor to suit your event’s theme and style. Whether it’s a wedding, reception or engagement, our stage setups and elegant decorations ensure a picture-perfect ambiance. Let our exceptional services and attention to detail make your celebration truly spectacular.

Haldi Stage

The Haldi ceremony is one of the most cherished pre-wedding rituals in Indian traditions, and we ensure it becomes a memorable celebration for you. Our venue offers beautifully designed stages adorned with vibrant yellow and floral decorations that perfectly capture the essence of this joyful occasion. From traditional marigold garlands to modern decorative elements, our customizable décor options create the perfect ambiance for your Haldi. Let us take care of every detail, so you can focus on enjoying this special moment with your loved ones.

Open Sky Lawn

Our open lawn offers a picturesque setting for your special celebrations, blending natural beauty with elegance. Surrounded by lush greenery and a serene atmosphere, it’s the perfect venue for receptions, cocktail parties, birthday parties, cultural programs or any outdoor event. With ample space to accommodate your guests, customizable décor options, and enchanting lighting setups, our lawn creates a magical ambiance under the open sky. Whether it’s a daytime function adorned with floral arrangements or an evening celebration illuminated by string lights we ensure every detail is tailored to your vision. Make your event truly unforgettable in our stunning open lawn setting.

Vidhi Mandap

Our Vidhi Mandap setup is designed to bring a touch of tradition and elegance to your sacred ceremonies. Featuring vibrant floral arrangements, and stunning chandeliers that add a divine glow, our mandap creates a serene and spiritual ambiance for your rituals. With comfortable seating, ample space for family and priests, and customizable décor to suit your preferences, our Vidhi Mandap sets the stage for timeless memories. Let us make your special moments as grand and meaningful as they deserve to be.
