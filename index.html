A Venue That Combines Luxury with Warm Hospitality!

Rajput Royals, one of the largest and most prestigious banquet halls in the Sangli-Miraj region. Sprawling across 7 acres of lush, serene surroundings, tucked away from the hustle and bustle of the city, yet easily accessible. Our venue is located in close proximity to the Miraj Bus Stand on the Ratnagiri-Nagpur Highway, offering a perfect blend of tranquility and accessibility. Ample parking space ensures your guests can enjoy the event with ease and convenience. We have a capacity to comfortably host a flexible number of guests. Rajput Royals is designed to host grand weddings, receptions, and other special celebrations. The venue features a beautifully designed haldi stage for traditional ceremonies, a spacious dining room, and a dedicated buffet section to cater to your culinary needs. For those looking to celebrate outdoors, our open lawn provides an ideal setting for memorable moments.
