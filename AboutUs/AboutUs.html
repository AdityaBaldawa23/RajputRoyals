What is the capacity of the hall?

Our hall can comfortably accommodate up to 1500 guests, with seating arrangements customizable based on your event needs.

Can we bring our own vendors?

Yes, you are welcome to bring your own vendors, but they must adhere to our guidelines.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we offer in-house catering services with a variety of menu options. You can choose from different packages based on your preferences and budget. Custom menu options are also available.

What are the available payment methods?

We accept payments via cash, credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. A deposit is required to secure your booking before the event.

Is parking available for guests?

Yes, we have ample parking space available, free of charge for your guests.

Can we have music or a DJ during the event?

Absolutely! You are welcome to have live music or a DJ. We can also provide recommendations for DJs who are familiar with our venue.

Is the venue accessible for people with disabilities?

Yes, our venue is fully accessible for people with disabilities, including ramps and accessible restrooms.

Are there any restrictions on decoration?

We allow most types of decorations but there are restrictions on open flames, confetti, and damage to walls or furniture. Please consult with our team to discuss your decoration plans.

Do you provide event coordination services?

Yes, we offer event coordination services to help ensure your wedding runs smoothly. Our team can assist with planning, scheduling, and managing vendors on the day of the event.

Are there accommodation facilities for guests?

Yes, we provide on-site accommodation options, including comfortable rooms and dormitories, to cater to your guests' needs. These are perfect for out-of-town attendees or those who wish to stay overnight after the event.

Is there a bridal suite available?

Yes, we provide a complimentary bridal suite for the couple to prepare and relax before the event.
